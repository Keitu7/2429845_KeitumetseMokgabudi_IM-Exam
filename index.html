ABOUT US

KLAVA was founded by a single vision — and a single woman — who believed that pastries should never have to choose between elegance and accessibility. As the sole owner of KLAVA, I wanted to create a pastry restaurant that takes the art of patisserie seriously… but without the intimidation, pretentiousness or unaffordable price tags that often come with it. KLAVA was born to be different — daring in flavour, playful in personality, and proudly sophisticated in its style.

Here, pastries are crafted with the intention to be both beautiful and attainable. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick sweet treat on-the-go, or settling in for a slow indulgent moment with a plated pastry that turns heads — KLAVA is designed for every type of appetite and every type of budget. We welcome everyone, at KLAVA, we believe elegance belongs to everyone.
